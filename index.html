What happened?

Early in the morning, on February 24, 2022, President Vladimir Putin delivered an address to the nation announcing the start of a special military operation in Donbas. In this appeal, the head of state refers to numerous requests for military assistance from the heads of the Donetsk and Lugansk People's Republics. Vladimir Putin said that the purpose of what is happening, according to him, is to protect people who are being subjected to genocide, as well as his concerns about statements by the Ukrainian authorities about the possibility of returning nuclear weapons and moving NATO forces to Russian borders. After a couple of hours, messages about explosions in Kyiv, Kharkov, Odessa began to appear massively on social networks, Mariupol and other Ukrainian cities.
